About Us

Since 2012, MyFlower Hotels has been a beacon of luxury in Erbil. With three branches - MyFlower 1, MyFlower 2, and MyFlower 3 - we offer 330 rooms of unparalleled comfort and elegance. Our mission is to provide top-notch hospitality services and create unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Rooms

Deluxe Room

Our Deluxe Rooms offer the perfect blend of luxury and comfort. Amenities include free Wi-Fi, a king-sized bed, and a stunning view of the city.

Suite

Our Suites provide a spacious and luxurious experience, featuring a separate living area, premium furnishings, and exclusive services.

Family Room

Perfect for families, our Family Rooms offer ample space, comfortable bedding, and a range of amenities to ensure a pleasant stay for everyone.
